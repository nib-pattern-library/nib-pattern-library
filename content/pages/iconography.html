Icons are used accross our site to help illustrate a concept, visually break up content and assist in navigation.

  • Icons can be in colour
  • Do not size icons less than 16px
  • Do not source any other icons (if new icons needed speak to design@nib.com.au)

For your designs, you can find the source *.svg files here.

Rotation

We rotate icons with css to reduce uneeded duplication, apply these classes to rotate icons where appropriate, rotation is always clockwise.

Particularly useful for the navigation set, where direction of the icon should imply what will happen. Consider using animation when rotating icons.
